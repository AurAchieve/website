Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. This page explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

What we collect

  • User content you create in the app: tasks, habits, study plans, and Social Blocker (online mode) settings.
  • Account and session basics: email, name, IP address, and device type.
  • Optional image uploads for task verification (when you choose to use this feature).

How we process and share

Some features rely on third‑party services:

  • Images may be processed by Google (Gemini) when you opt into image‑based verification. Google’s privacy policies apply to that processing.
  • We use Appwrite Cloud to host and store your data (Frankfurt, Germany). Appwrite’s privacy policy may apply.

We do not sell your data. Data in transit is encrypted. Passwords are hashed and salted; we cannot view your password.

Your choices

  • Access and update your account details in the app.
  • Request account and data deletion.
